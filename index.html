📖 Torpedo Strategy Guide

⚡ Quick Facts

  • Speed: 50 meters/second
  • Range: 15km+
  • Valid targets: Needlefish, Swordfish, and Carriers

🎯 Optimal Firing

  • Best Position: Fire torpedoes broadside to your hull
  • Avoid: Firing along bow/stern (adds 20s turn time and 750m error)
  • Ocean currents affect torpedo trajectory

⚙️ Arming Strategy

  • Ideal arming point: 1000m before target
  • Why? Bypasses enemy noisemaker defenses
  • Calculate delay: (Distance - 1000m) ÷ 50 = Activation Delay in seconds

⚠️ Enemy Defenses

  • Carriers & Swordfish: Always have noisemaker defenses
  • Needlefish: May have noisemaker capability
